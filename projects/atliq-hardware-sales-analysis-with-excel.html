AtliQ Hardware Sales Analysis with Excel

Created on July 16, 2025

Tools : MS Excel • Power Query • Power Pivot • Pivot Tables

Project Description

AtliQ Hardware is a B2B tech company serving major retailers like Croma, Flipkart, and Best Buy across India, NA, ROA, SE, NE, and ANZ through Retail, Distributor, and Direct Sales channels.

I built a comprehensive Sales & Financial Analytics Dashboard in Excel to help AtliQ track performance, optimize costs, and focus on high-performing regions and customers.

Project Goals

  • ✔ Analyze monthly and yearly P&L reports
  • ✔ Evaluate customer performance and profitability
  • ✔ Compare market performance across regions
  • ✔ Support strategic decision-making with data insights

Project Evaluation

P&L Report (Fiscal Yearly)

A year-over-year financial summary showing steady revenue growth from ₹36.2Cr to ₹54.1Cr and increasing net profit, enabling long-term strategy planning.

P&L Report (Monthly)

Revealed seasonal revenue patterns and expense spikes. March had the highest revenue (₹5.6Cr), while July flagged high costs needing attention.

P&L 2021 for Markets

Highlighted regional profitability. India led with ₹16.9Cr revenue, while ROA showed the best gross margin efficiency.

Market Performance Report

Compared market-wise volume and value. India had the highest unit sales (73,985), but smaller zones like SE/NE offered high-value sales opportunities.

Customer Performance Report

Identified top clients like Best Buy and Flipkart, revealing revenue dependency and prompting a focus on customer diversification.

GM% by Quarters (Sub-Zone)

Tracked profitability trends. India’s margin fell from ~42% to ~32%, signaling the need for pricing and cost control strategies.
